Who We Are

Discover the heart and soul behind our movement for unity and peace

Our Story

The journey that brought us together to create a movement for peace and unity

2018

The Beginning

A small group of community leaders came together after witnessing increasing division and tribalism in our neighborhoods. We started with weekly dialogues in a community center.

2020

Growing Together

During challenging times, our movement gained momentum as more people sought connection and understanding. We launched our first Unity Festival with 500 participants.

2023

National Recognition

Amani Umoja Kwetu was recognized by the Ministry of Social Services for our peace-building efforts. We expanded to 12 regional chapters across Kenya.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our work every day

Our Vision

A nation united across all lines of division, where communities thrive through mutual respect, understanding, and collaboration.

Our Mission

To build bridges between communities by fostering dialogue, promoting understanding, and creating spaces for shared experiences.

Our Purpose

To create a movement that empowers individuals to become agents of peace and unity in their communities.

Core Values

The foundation of everything we do

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our actions and decisions.

Equality

We believe every person deserves equal respect and opportunity regardless of background.

Dialogue

We create safe spaces for open and honest conversations that build bridges.

Compassion

We approach all interactions with empathy and understanding.
