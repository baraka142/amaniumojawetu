Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our movement

How can I get involved with Amani Umoja Kwetu?

There are several ways to get involved! You can attend our community dialogues, volunteer for events, share your story, or become a member. Visit our Join Us page for more details.

Do I need any special qualifications to join?

No special qualifications are needed! We welcome anyone who believes in our mission of promoting unity and rejecting division. All you need is an open heart and willingness to engage in constructive dialogue.

How can I share my story of unity?

We'd love to hear your story! You can share it through our Our Stories page, or send it to us via email at stories@amaniumojakwetu.org. We may feature it on our website or social media.

Can I invite Amani Umoja Kwetu to speak at my event?

Absolutely! We're available for speaking engagements, workshops, and community events. Please contact us at events@amaniumojakwetu.org with details about your event.

How can I support your work financially?

As a new movement, we're currently building our support systems. Please contact us at donations@amaniumojakwetu.org for information on how to support our work financially.
