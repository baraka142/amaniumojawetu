Reconciliation in Kibera: From Neighbors to Friends

"After the elections, our community was deeply divided along tribal lines. We stopped speaking to neighbors we'd known for years. Through Amani Umoja Kwetu's dialogue circles, we learned to listen to each other's fears and hopes. Today, we're not just neighbors again - we're family."

Jane Wambui

Kibera, Nairobi
