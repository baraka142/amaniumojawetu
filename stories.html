“From Division to Dialogue” – Asha’s Journey

Growing up in a community divided by tribal lines, Asha never imagined she’d one day lead peace circles in her village. After attending an Amani Umoja Kwetu dialogue session, she found the courage to speak up — not with anger, but with empathy.

Today, she mentors young girls and helps bridge gaps between families that once refused to speak to each other.

“Unity isn’t just a dream — it’s something we build, one conversation at a time.”
